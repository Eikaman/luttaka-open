Data Deletion Instructions

At Flowcore Conference App, we understand that your privacy is important. If you wish to delete your account and personal data associated with it, please follow the instructions below.

Instructions for Deleting Your Data:

  1. Log into your account.
  2. Go to your profile settings.
  3. Click on "Delete Account" or similar option.
  4. Confirm your choice.

If you are unable to delete your account by the above method or have any other questions about data deletion, please contact us at Flowcore Sp/f, Vestara Bryggja 15, 100 Tórshavn, Faroe Islands, Phone +298 556600, www.flowcore.com, flowcore@flowcore.com and we will assist you with the process.

Please note that after deleting your account, you will no longer have access to Flowcore Conference App and all your data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
